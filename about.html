About Green Lies Detector

Our mission is to make corporate environmental claims verifiable and hold corporations accountable.

Our Story

Green Lies Detector began as an academic project and has grown into a practical tool combining web scraping, NLP, sentiment analysis, and custom heuristics to detect greenwashing.

Approach

  • Automated claim collection
  • Transformer-based context understanding
  • Custom scoring and transparency reports

Impact

Our goal: raise awareness and empower regulators, journalists, and consumers to call out misleading sustainability claims.
